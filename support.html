Support

How can we help?

For support, email vivapantry@outlook.com. Include the email address you use in VivaPantry and a short description of the issue.

Login

Need help signing in, confirming email, or accessing your account? Send the account email and what happened.

Receipts

For receipt scanning issues, include whether the scan failed, extracted incorrect items, or did not update prices.

Meal plans

If meal planning is not working as expected, include the goal, household preferences, and any visible error message.

Grocery lists

Tell us if items are missing, duplicated, not syncing, or not matching your meal plan.

Account deletion

Use the account deletion page for deletion instructions and required information.

General feedback

Feature ideas, confusing flows, and reliability reports are welcome while VivaPantry prepares for launch.
